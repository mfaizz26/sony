About Sony

Sony was founded in 1946 in Tokyo by Masaru Ibuka and Akio Morita. It started as a small electronics shop and grew into one of the world’s leading tech and entertainment corporations. From pioneering the transistor radio to the Walkman and now the PlayStation, Sony has defined generations of technology and entertainment.

Today, Sony is a global leader in electronics, gaming, music, film, and financial services. With innovation at its core, Sony's mission is to fill the world with emotion through the power of creativity and technology.

Our Services

  • Gaming: Home to PlayStation, Sony is a titan in interactive entertainment with consoles, VR systems, and a massive online ecosystem.

  • Financial Services: Offers banking, insurance, and AI-powered fintech in Japan through its financial subsidiaries.
